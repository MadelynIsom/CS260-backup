Hi! I'm Madelyn.

When I was little, I loved making faces at the camera and seeing myself frozen in time. I would often roleplay with my siblings and capture key moments in our fantasies. For my 10th birthday, I got a small dslr camera. I haven't stopped taking pictures since. My goal is to help you capture the same kinds of magical moments with the people that matter most. Precious memories that will last a lifetime.
